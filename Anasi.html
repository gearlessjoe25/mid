Early Awakening

At age seven, Anasi Banyan IV discovered a hidden crawlspace in his family garage containing ancient books and a mysterious ring. When he wore it, he briefly communed with his ancestors for the first time‚Äîa transformative awakening that drew him toward the supernatural. Fascinated, he immersed himself in occult knowledge, sharing discoveries with his siblings and persistently seeking rare texts with the help of a local library intern, Angela Wilson, forging a lasting friendship.

The Banyan Legacy

Years later, Anasi tracked a legendary dagger linked to the Banyan lineage, rumored to be in Toronto near his estranged grandfather. He persuaded his hesitant father to visit, and together with his siblings, planned to reclaim it. Their quest was threatened by a bitter relative, Leon Trigrunti, who sought the dagger to restore stolen family power. In the ensuing struggle, only Anasi could truly wield the weapon, which chose him. Leon fled, abducting Anasi's brother Ogun, who was never seen again.

Scholar and Hunter

Haunted by loss, Anasi focused on solo research while maintaining close ties with his sister and Angela. He married Ohema, fathered twins, and navigated the balance between family life and the supernatural. His expertise eventually drew the attention of the Monster Investigation Division (M.I.D.), who recruited him as an agent. Under the cover of a librarian, Anasi now operates at the intersection of scholarship, family duty, and the unceasing threats of the supernatural world.
